Booking questions, answered

What am I paying today?

Just the refundable deposit - £150 for a 20ft container or £75 for an 8ft. It secures your unit and is refunded in full when you leave, provided the unit is left as it was found. Your monthly rental is invoiced separately by email.

Is the payment secure?

Yes - payments are handled by Stripe, one of the world's largest payment providers. Your card details go directly to Stripe and never touch our website.

What happens after I book?

You'll get an instant confirmation email, and we'll be in touch - usually the same day - to confirm your move-in, send your first invoice and sort your padlock and mobile phone gate access.

How far ahead can I book?

Up to 3 days in advance. Units come and go quickly, so we only take firm bookings we know we can honour. Planning further ahead? Get an instant quote and we'll be in touch nearer the time.

What if I change my mind?

No problem - there's no long-term contract, and if you decide not to go ahead before moving in, just call us and we'll refund your deposit.
