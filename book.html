Booking questions, answered

What am I paying today?

Two things, in one payment: your refundable deposit (£150 for a 20ft container, £75 for an 8ft), plus your first rent payment - the rest of the current month, worked out daily from your move-in date. For example, move in on the 17th of July and your rent covers the 17th to the 31st. The deposit is refunded in full when you leave, provided the unit is left as it was found.

Moving in within 7 days of the end of the month? Your first payment also covers the following month, so we're not invoicing you again days after you've just paid.

Choosing to pay 6 or 12 months upfront? Then there's nothing to pay by card today - we'll be in touch as soon as possible with your invoice at the discounted rate. Note that a booking request doesn't reserve your unit: units go to whoever pays first, so yours is secured once your invoice is paid.

When can I move in, and when is the next payment?

You're fully paid up the moment you book - so once we've confirmed your unit and sorted your padlock and gate access, you can move in, often the same day. Your next payment isn't until the 1st of the following month, invoiced by email as normal.

Is the payment secure?

Yes - payments are handled by Stripe, one of the world's largest payment providers. Your card details go directly to Stripe and never touch our website.

What happens after I book?

You'll get an instant confirmation email, and we'll be in touch - usually the same day - to confirm your move-in, send your first invoice and sort your padlock and mobile phone gate access.

How far ahead can I book?

Up to 3 days in advance. Units come and go quickly, so we only take firm bookings we know we can honour. Planning further ahead? Get in touch - call 07375 355233 or email info@mbstorage.co.uk - and we'll see what we can arrange.

What if I change my mind?

No problem - there's no long-term contract, and if you decide not to go ahead before moving in, just call us and we'll refund your payment.
