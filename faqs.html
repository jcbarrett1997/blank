Help & FAQs

Storage questions, answered

Everything you need to know about sizes, security, access and booking. Can't find your answer? Call 07375 355233 - a real person, whenever you need one.

How much does storage cost?

Our containers are billed on a simple flat monthly rate with no long tie-in. Use the instant quote tool for a price by email in minutes, or call 07375 355233. Prefer to pay ahead? Paying 6 or 12 months upfront saves you up to 10%.

What sizes do you offer?

Two container sizes - a 20ft × 8ft unit (~160 sq ft, about the size of a single garage) and an 8ft × 6ft 6in unit (~52 sq ft). We also have indoor warehouse units up to 2,700 sq ft at our Batley site. See the 20ft and 8ft size guides.

Is there a minimum contract?

You’re only tied in if you want to be. There’s a one-month minimum, then it’s rolling monthly - just give 14 days’ notice when you’re ready to leave, and your deposit is refunded when you go. If you’d rather commit for longer, paying 6 or 12 months upfront saves you up to 10%.

Can I access my unit any time?

Yes - you access the yard 24/7 using our mobile phone gate entry system, so you never need to wait for staff. Just dial from your mobile and the gates open.

How secure is the site?

Every site has 24/7 motion-sensing CCTV, electric or secured gates and mobile-phone-controlled entry. Every unit is secured with a high-quality padlock that we supply - for site security, only MB Storage padlocks are used.

Do I need my own padlock?

No - we provide a high-quality padlock with every unit, so your unit is secured from day one at no extra cost. For consistent site security, only MB Storage padlocks are used, so there’s no need to buy your own.

What can I store?

Household items, business stock, tools and equipment, vehicles where permitted, and more. Prohibited items are the usual ones - no perishable, hazardous, flammable or illegal goods. If you’re not sure whether something is suitable, just ask and we’ll be happy to advise.

Do I need insurance?

We recommend insuring the contents of your unit - your belongings remain your responsibility while in storage. If you’d like guidance on cover, give us a call and we’ll point you in the right direction.

Where are you located?

Three West Yorkshire sites - Batley (Grange Road), Liversedge (Wormald Street) and Brighouse (Calder Street, run by our sister company JB Storage Solutions).

How do I book?

Get an instant quote online and we’ll email you a price. Reserve your unit and move in - most customers are set up the same week.

Still have a question?

Get an instant quote by email, or get in touch and we'll help you find the right unit.
