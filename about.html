Who we are

MB Storage is a family-run self storage business based in Batley, West Yorkshire, with sites in Batley, Liversedge and Brighouse. We built MB Storage around one simple idea: storage should be secure, easy and honest.

That's why most of our units are brand new - and all are clean and well-maintained - every site is watched by motion-sensing cameras that alert us 24/7, and every customer gets access to our mobile phone entry system, so you can get to your things whenever you need them, not just when an office is open.
